Our Selection Rationale

Not Just Customer Support. The Face of Rubicon's Client Experience.

Rubicon's mission to end waste starts with every client interaction. The Client Service Associate is the person who makes that mission tangible for the businesses Rubicon serves. This role needs someone who can handle high-volume communication, resolve issues proactively, and represent Rubicon's values in every touchpoint. Transparency, responsiveness, and a genuine service orientation are non-negotiable.

We filtered for candidates with proven experience in bilingual customer service environments, strong CRM proficiency, and a track record of handling complex client interactions without supervision. We prioritized people who've worked in fast-paced, multi-channel support roles (phone, email, chat, ticketing systems) and who understand the rhythm of keeping clients informed while managing competing priorities.

Every candidate in this batch has experience supporting U.S. clients remotely, speaks fluent English and native Spanish, and has demonstrated the capacity to take ownership of client outcomes, not just tickets.

What we screened for: Bilingual fluency with professional English, CRM and ticketing system experience (Salesforce, Zendesk, or equivalent), history of high-volume support (40+ daily interactions), conflict resolution skills, and the ability to work autonomously in remote settings. We also weighted candidates who showed initiative in process improvement and documentation.
